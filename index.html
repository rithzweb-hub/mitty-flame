Our Story

Mitti & Flame is born from a passion for traditional Indian cooking methods combined with contemporary dining sophistication. Each dish is a testament to the centuries-old art of clay-pot preparation.

Our name reflects our philosophy: "Mitti" (earth) represents the earthen clay vessels that define our cooking, while "Flame" symbolizes the controlled fire that transforms ingredients into culinary poetry.

We source the finest regional ingredients and employ time-honored techniques passed down through generations, bringing you an authentic experience unlike any other.

Rooted in tradition. Crafted for today.
